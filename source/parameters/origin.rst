Origin Parameter
########################
The Origin Parameter is on the |Group_Layer|, |Rotate_Layer|, |Translate_Layer| and |Scale_Layer| layers. It is used has center of layer's related operation : to translate the entire canvas referenced by the Group Layer, has center for Rotate and Scale layers ...
